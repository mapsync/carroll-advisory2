Each year, Kentucky Rural Water Association recognizes water and wastewater utilities that have made substantial and lasting improvements in customer service, drinking water & wastewater services, community support, operations and regulatory compliance.

This recognition reflects the commitment of our entire CCWD team. Every employee plays a vital role in ensuring our customers receive dependable service while continually improving our system for today and the future.
