Remember to protect those pipes to avoid a major headache later. With the frigid temperatures, please remember to do the following through the night and even during the DAY!!!!

\* Make sure you know where you Shutoff Valve is located in your home.

\* Leave a slow drip in bathroom or kitchen, which ever is the furthest away from the meter.

\* Open up cabinet doors to let the warmth in your home to warm those pipes a little more.
