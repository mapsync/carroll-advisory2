Join us in helping the Food Pantry of Carroll County feed those among us in need this holiday season. We will be collecting non-perishable food items in our office now through November 14th, 2025. All donations are appreciated, but are especially in need of the following:

\* Beef Stew

\* Tuna

\* Chicken Noodle Soup

\* Peanut Butter & Jelly

\* Canned- Corn, Green Beans, Peas & Potatoes

While you can drop your donations off at our office, you can also drop donations off at the following places in Carroll County:

\* First National Bank

\* Carrollton Federal Bank

\* Carroll County Public Library

\* Food Pantry @ 113 3rd Street Carrollton, KY 41008

\* Cash Donations can be dropped off @ Crawford & Baxter Law Office @ 523 Highland Ave Carrollton, KY 41008

Thank you for helping those less fortunate this Holiday season!!!
