Kentucky Division of Water proudly celebrates the hard work and dedication of Kentucky's Water and Wastewater professionals in providing safe, high quality drinking water to communities across the state! Newsweek recently recognized Kentucky as one of the top states for water quality, ranking just behind Hawaii and Delaware.

This achievement is a testament to the commitment of operators, utilities, and industry professionals who work tirelessly to protect and maintain Kentucky's precious water resources.

If you see our operators out and about this month, shout a Thank you for their efforts!
