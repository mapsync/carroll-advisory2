A Boil Water Advisory is being issued by Carroll County Water District #1 until further notice for the following customers located in Owen County, located on Flamingo Ln, Greenmeadow Dr, Bridgehaven, Heatherhill, Riverside Dr, and Fairbourne Ln.

This is a precautionary measure and it is recommended that you boil the water for 3 minutes prior to consumption.

This advisory is until further notice. If you have any questions please call 502-347-9500. Thank you for your understanding and we do apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.
