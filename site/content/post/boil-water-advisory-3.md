A Boil Water Advisory is being issued by Carroll County Water District #1 until further notice of customers located in between 5000-7000 block of Hwy 36 E in Ghent and Lanham Lane. This is a precautionary measure and it is recommended that you boil the water used for drinking at a rolling boil for 3 minutes prior to consumption. This advisory is until further notice. If you have any questions or comments, please call  502-347-9500. Thank you for understanding and we do apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.
