To avoid having late charges assessed to your account payments must be processed by no later than August 20, 2022.  Our office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00-4:00, closed 12:00-12:30 for lunch.  You can pay from this app, online at Carrollcountywater.com, or by calling 1-866-612-4915.  If you have not received your August bill card please feel free to call our office at 502-347-9500 to find out your balance due. 

*\*If you have already paid your water bill, please disregard. \**
