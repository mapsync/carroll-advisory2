message: >-
  Remember to protect those pipes to avoid a major headache later. With the
  upcoming frigid temperatures, please remember to do the following through the
  night and even during the DAY!!!!


  * Make sure you know where your Shutoff Valve is located in your home.

  * Leave a slow drip in bathroom or kitchen, whichever is the furthest away from the meter.

  * Open up cabinet doors to let the warmth in your home to warm those pipes a little more.

  *When cold weather hits, if at all possible please keep the lid on the meter pit to avoid a flash freeze.
